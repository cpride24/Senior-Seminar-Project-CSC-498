Meet Our Coaches

Michael Hayes

He originally from London England serves as Tennis Director here at Jackson Country club. Michael brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to the tennis program from having the opportunity to work with the same coaches as Federer, Murray and Sampras as well as having played and practiced with former world #1 players Caroline Wozniacki and Victoria Azarenka. Michael played college tennis for the University of Memphis where he earned his Masters degree in Exercise Science. Michael was a top 10 nationally ranked junior, having a career win over a top 10 U18’s player in the world. Michael is a USPTA Elite teaching professional, an ITPA Tennis Performance Specialist and a certified Cardio Tennis Instructor.

Jay Veazey

serves as Head tennis professional and has taught tennis here at JCC for the last 30 years, formerly as the Director. Jay played college tennis for Lambuth reaching the conference finals, in both singles and doubles, as well as being voted player of the year in 1993. Jay has great experience in developing players of all levels, having developed numerous state finalists and college level players, and has been recognized by the USPTA for his years of service.

Dasha Klyuyenko

She serves as assistant tennis professional is originally from the Ukraine played #1 for Bethel College tennis team. Dasha is USPTA certified and serves as an assistant with our junior program as well as providing individual lessons.

Fran Chandler

He serves as our tournament hitter. Fran brings great experience as she is former number 1 in the world player and continues to compete tournaments all over the world.

JCC Tennis Instruction

  • All 3 of our USPTA certified pro strongly emphasize modern offensive ball striking techniques to enable our juniors and adults to reach their maximum potential!
  • We start all of our students with pro level technique from the beginning so there is nothing to be changed later. We don’t take short cuts which will limit potential later to “just get the ball in play now”!
  • We put a strong focus on running proven and statistically sound patterns of play for both singles and doubles.
  • The average point lasts under four shots all the way from 12’s juniors to the top players in the world so we strongly incorporate serve + 1 and return + 1 shot combinations.

  • Our program has produced 30 players who have reached the state finals or played college tennis.
  • Our pros utilize the most advanced training tools available including: I-playmate ball machines able to produce any shot combinations while the pro can stay on the same side of the court as the students, use of Hudl Technique slow motion video which enable us to compare you side by side with the top pros in the world on a frame by frame basis, both indoor courts have full court video capability to be able to watch your lesson or view your point plays and matches, and use of an elevated serving machine to allow for realistic practice of return of serves, and finally our pros also incorporate appropriate and modern hand feeding and ball feeding techniques to push the students continually.

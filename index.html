We have a wide offering of junior and adult clinics ranging from 3-4 years old to USTA tournament players to 4.0 ladies to cardio tennis. We can service all of your tennis needs here at JCC whether you are just looking to hit some balls, get an intense workout, instructional doubles or singles clinics, beginner lessons, 3 year olds tiny tots program, advanced technical and tactical lessons, social events or USTA tournament training. We really promote the long term development for all of our players and do not place the emphasis on winning straight away. In our junior clinics we really focus on using step by step progressions to help develop each student and to help them move into the next class when they’re ready.

Everything we teach in our clinics comes from the best teaching methods in the world, which is backed up by science, and the most up to date statistical data regarding tactics and strategy. As the majority of tennis points (60-70%) last less than 4 shots we put heavy focus first strike tennis and use the data from the worlds leading tennis strategist Craig O’Shannessy. We work on tactics and strategies for all 3 phases of the point but put an emphasis on the first phase with the serve +1 and return +1 patterns of play.

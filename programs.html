Programs

Here are the 5 levels of our junior development program.

  • Tiny Tots

    This class is for 3-5 year olds and is a great athletic skill building class. We introduce the basics of tennis as well as building athleticism and motor skills such as running, jumping, sidestepping, balance, throwing, catching, and tracking. This is a great class to build a strong athletic foundation that is necessary for developing more advanced skills in the future.

  • Orange ball Big Hitters

    This class is for 4 -7 year olds who can demonstrate the basic tennis strokes and can make solid contact with the ball consistently. The focus is now more on developing the strokes. These juniors are able to participate in the clinic without parental help.

  • Junior Development I

    This class is for 6-10 year olds who have solid fundamental strokes. These juniors are ready for competitive point plays but not yet ready for match play. We place the emphasis on sound stroke production and being able to create a solid ball strike.

  • Junior Development II

    This class is for 8-12 year olds who are ready for USTA orange, USTA green or middle school players. The emphasis is on hitting the correct ball strike to targets on the move and developing serve and returns.

  • USTA Tournament and High School Class

    This is for juniors who are looking to compete in USTA tournaments or make the top of their school teams. These juniors should want to play USTA tournaments and be able to play with focused intensity during clinic, point plays and matchplay. The emphasis is on footwork, fitness, developing a solid base game and developing their serve +1 and return +1 patterns of play.

  • Adult Tennis Clinics

    Jackson Country Club also offers a wide range of ladies clinics and individual instruction. From the complete beginner to the advanced 4.5 level player, to cardio tennis and social events, our Professional Tennis staff can provide all of your tennis needs.

  • Clinic and Plays: 2.5, 3.0/3.5, 4.0/4.5

    In these weekly clinic and plays we run drills focusing on specific tactical and strategical situations followed by point plays. We limit all clinics to 12 participants so that we maintain a 4:1 student ratio. We use the iplaymate ball machine that allows for a significantly increased feed rate so players get to hit double the amount of balls than hand fed.

  • Cardio Tennis

    Our tennis workout class consists of high energy hitting drills and tennis movements. This is great for those who want to hit a lot of balls without technical instruction. The workout is open to all levels, male or female and will be sure to burn calories in a fun group environment!
